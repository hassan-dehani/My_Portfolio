Data Collection & Cleaning

High-quality analysis begins with high-quality data. I collect data from Excel, CSV, relational databases (SQL Server, MySQL, PostgreSQL), APIs, and—when required—via responsible web scraping. After collection I perform thorough validation and cleaning to ensure data is consistent, accurate and analysis-ready.

  • What I do: source mapping, automated imports, validation rules, detect & handle missing values, deduplication, type casting and format normalization.
  • Tools & techniques: Python (Pandas, NumPy), Power Query, Excel, SQL scripts, API clients.
  • Output: clean datasets, documented transform steps, and reproducible import pipelines.

Key benefits: faster analysis, fewer errors downstream, improved model accuracy, and clear data lineage so you can trust every insight.

Data Analysis & Exploration

I carry out Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to uncover patterns, correlations and actionable intelligence. My approach combines descriptive statistics, segmentation, trend analysis and hypothesis testing to surface the story hidden in your data.

  • What I do: statistical summaries, correlation matrices, segmentation, anomaly detection and hypothesis-driven investigation.
  • Tools & techniques: Python (Pandas, SciPy), Excel, SQL, Power BI.
  • Output: analysis reports, prioritized insights, and recommendations for next steps.

Key benefits: identify growth opportunities, spot inefficiencies, and get evidence-based recommendations for business decisions.

Data Visualization

I translate complex data into clear, actionable visualizations and dashboards that non-technical stakeholders can understand at a glance. Visual storytelling is designed to highlight KPIs, trends and exceptions.

  • What I do: design dashboards and charts (trend lines, heatmaps, KPI cards, drill-downs, and interactive filters).
  • Tools & techniques: Power BI, Tableau, Excel dashboards, Plotly/Matplotlib/Seaborn for custom visuals.
  • Output: reusable, automated dashboards and visual reports tuned to the audience (executive, operational, analyst).

Key benefits: faster insight consumption, better stakeholder alignment, and visuals that drive action.

Business Insights & Reporting

I deliver automated reporting and strategic insights—combining data analysis with business context. Reports track KPIs, present trend analysis and give prioritized recommendations to support strategic decisions.

  • What I do: build scheduled reports (daily/weekly/monthly), KPI monitoring, performance dashboards and executive summaries.
  • Tools & techniques: Power BI, Google Data Studio, Excel automation, Python scripts for ETL and alerting.
  • Output: automated report deliveries, stakeholder-ready slide decks and action plans.

Key benefits: consistent visibility into business performance, reduced manual reporting overhead, and clearer, faster decision-making.

Data Modeling & Forecasting

I build robust predictive models and time-series forecasts to help plan inventory, sales, staffing, and financials. Models are validated and explained in plain language so stakeholders can act with confidence.

  • What I do: regression, classification, time-series forecasting, cross-validation, model selection and feature engineering.
  • Tools & techniques: Python (scikit-learn, statsmodels), cross-validation workflows, error-metric analysis and model explainability.
  • Output: deployable models, forecast sheets, scenario planning and documented assumptions.

Key benefits: anticipate demand, reduce risk, improve resource planning and base decisions on measured predictions.

Database & Query Services

I design and optimize database structures and queries so your systems return results quickly and reliably—even with large datasets. Proper schema design and indexing make dashboards and reports fast and scalable.

  • What I do: schema design, normalization, indexing, optimized SQL queries, views and stored procedures.
  • Tools & techniques: Microsoft SQL Server, MySQL, PostgreSQL, query profiling and tuning.
  • Output: efficient query sets, documentation, and improved run-times for analytics workloads.

Key benefits: faster data access, reliable joins/aggregations, and a database that grows with your needs.

Custom Analysis Projects

If you have a specific question or business challenge, I deliver tailored analysis and actionable results. Projects range from marketing campaign evaluation and customer segmentation to financial analysis and operational optimization.

  • What I do: tailored project scoping, custom analysis (A/B testing, cohort analysis), and solution delivery (reports, dashboards or code).
  • Tools & techniques: Excel, Python, Power BI, and SQL depending on the project needs.
  • Output: clear deliverables, prioritized recommendations and reproducible notebooks or dashboards.

Key benefits: answers to your unique business questions, practical recommendations, and deliverables that stakeholders can use immediately.

Data Documentation & Training

I create clear documentation and deliver training so your team can maintain, understand and extend analytics and dashboards without dependency on outside help.

  • What I do: document pipelines, data dictionaries, transformation steps, and model assumptions; deliver live or recorded training sessions.
  • Tools & techniques: step-by-step documentation, training slides, hands-on exercises in Power BI/Excel/Python.
  • Output: user manuals, how-to guides, and trained team members ready to operate your analytics stack.

Key benefits: team independence, better knowledge transfer, and reduced long-term support costs.
